Character Summary
Baron The Baron(ess) is a haughty individual and generally unpleasant to be around. They are also the type of person who tends to get what they want, even when it means expending the lives of those around them.
Arch Duke Though rich and classy, the Archduke(duchess) is perhaps the most down to Earth of the royal family, and not opposed to interacting with peasants.
